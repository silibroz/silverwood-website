ABOUT THE PROPERTY

Where History Meets The Himalayan Wilderness

Kothi Heritage Retreat in Chakung is unlike any other property in the Silverwood collection. A deeply personal stay with just 4 accommodations — 3 cottages and a treehouse — set within a landscape of extraordinary natural and cultural heritage.

The crown of the property is our 200-year-old classic heritage cottage, one of the oldest standing structures in the region. For those who want to be closer to the canopy, the treehouse offers a magical forest experience unlike anything else.
