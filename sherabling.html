ABOUT THE PROPERTY

Refined Stays in the Capital of Sikkim

The Sherabling offers a blend of deluxe and standard accommodations in the heart of Gangtok. With 10 rooms spread across two categories, it delivers a comfortable and accessible Himalayan stay.

Deluxe rooms feature king-size beds with modern amenities. Four of the deluxe rooms face the roadside and are equipped with fans. Standard rooms offer queen beds for a comfortable and budget-friendly option.
