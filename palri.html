ABOUT THE PROPERTY

Every Room Faces Kanchenjunga

Palri Akharot in Pelling is a rare gem — every single room in the property offers a direct, unobstructed view of the Kanchenjunga range, the world's third highest peak. This is not a feature, it is the promise.

With 10 rooms across three thoughtfully curated categories — Deluxe, Premium Deluxe, and Family Deluxe — each with super king beds and most with private balconies, Palri Akharot is designed for those who come to truly be with the mountains.
