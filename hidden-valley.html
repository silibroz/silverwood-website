ABOUT THE PROPERTY

A Retreat At The Edge of North Sikkim

The Hidden Valley in Lachung sits deep in the folds of North Sikkim, surrounded by dramatic high-altitude landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and pristine river valleys. It is the gateway to Yumthang and Zero Point — two of India's most breathtaking destinations.

With 13 rooms across deluxe and standard categories, The Hidden Valley offers a warm, intimate base for high-altitude explorations, blending natural splendour with genuine Lachungpa hospitality.
