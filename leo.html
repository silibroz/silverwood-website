ABOUT THE PROPERTY

Gangtok's Largest Silverwood Property

Hotel Leo International is the most expansive property in the Silverwood Gangtok collection, offering 26 rooms across standard and deluxe categories. A wide variety of room configurations caters to solo travellers, couples, families, and groups.

From queen-bedded balcony rooms to super king suites and family configurations, Leo International delivers flexibility and comfort at scale, with the warmth of Sikkimese hospitality.
