ABOUT THE PROPERTY

Traditional Comfort Near The Heart of Gangtok

The Mazong Hotel offers a warm, traditionally styled stay in a prime location near Vajra Stand — one of Gangtok's most convenient transport hubs. Easy access to taxis, shared vehicles, and the city centre makes it ideal for explorers.

With 8 thoughtfully appointed rooms, the property delivers comfort and character, blending local hospitality with the charm of classic Sikkimese architecture.
